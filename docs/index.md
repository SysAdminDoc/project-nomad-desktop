# N.O.M.A.D. Field Desk

**N.O.M.A.D. Field Desk** is an offline-first preparedness and field-operations desktop app.  
Everything — AI chat, encyclopedias, maps, medical records, supply tracking — runs fully offline after initial setup.

---

## Features at a Glance

- **Fully offline** after setup — no cloud, no subscriptions, no telemetry
- **AI Chat** powered by local LLMs (Llama, Mistral, Phi) via llama.cpp
- **Information Library** — offline Wikipedia, medical references, survival guides
- **Offline Maps** — downloadable tile packs for your region
- **25-tab Preparedness workspace** — inventory, medical, power, comms, security, and more
- **Interactive Decision Guides** — 13 step-by-step guides with 300+ decision nodes
- **LAN multi-device** — family/team members connect via local network browser
