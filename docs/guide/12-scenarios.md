## 12. Training Scenarios

Practice your emergency response with 4 realistic multi-phase simulations in the **Tools** tab:

| Scenario | Phases | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Grid Down — 7 Days | 7 | Progressive power failure: water loss, food decisions, security, medical, communications, recovery |
| Medical Crisis | 5 | Trauma response: assessment, bleeding control, pain management, monitoring, complications |
| Evacuation Under Threat | 5 | Emergency departure: warning, packing, route decisions, roadblocks, arrival |
| Winter Storm Survival | 5 | Cold weather: heating crisis, fuel rationing, pipe burst, neighbor aid, rescue |

If the AI is running, it generates realistic complications between phases based on your actual inventory and contacts data. Each run is scored 0-100 with an after-event review that identifies what you did well and what to improve.
