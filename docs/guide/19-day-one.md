## 19. Day One Checklist

If you just installed NOMAD and want to be operational as fast as possible, follow this checklist:

| Priority | Task | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Run the Setup Wizard — choose your storage drive and Essential content | 2 min |
| 2 | Install AI Chat from the Home page | 3 min |
| 3 | Download a small AI model (Llama 3.2 3B or Phi 3 Mini) | 5 min |
| 4 | Add your household members to Contacts (name, phone, blood type) | 5 min |
| 5 | Add your critical supplies to Inventory (water, food, medical, batteries) | 15 min |
| 6 | Set daily usage rates on consumable items (water, food staples) | 5 min |
| 7 | Fill out the Family Emergency Plan (meeting points, evacuation routes) | 10 min |
| 8 | Download your regional map for offline navigation | 5 min |
| 9 | Complete at least one built-in Checklist (72-Hour Kit recommended) | 10 min |
| 10 | Run through one Interactive Guide (Water Purification is a good start) | 5 min |

> **Result:** After completing these 10 steps (~65 minutes), you will have a functional offline command center with AI chat, offline maps, supply tracking with automated alerts, and a family emergency plan. Your Readiness Score should jump to at least a C or B grade.
