## 20. Choosing the Right AI Model

AI models vary in size, speed, and quality. Here is a guide to help you choose:

| Model | Size | RAM Needed | Best For | Speed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Phi 3 Mini** | 2.3 GB | 4 GB | Quick answers, older PCs, limited RAM | Fast |
| **Llama 3.2 3B** | 2.0 GB | 4 GB | General questions, good balance of speed and quality | Fast |
| **Gemma 3 4B** | 3.3 GB | 6 GB | Better reasoning, multilingual support | Medium |
| **Mistral 7B** | 4.1 GB | 8 GB | High-quality answers, good for medical and technical questions | Medium |
| **Llama 3.1 8B** | 4.7 GB | 8 GB | Best overall quality for 8GB systems | Medium |
| **Qwen3 8B** | 4.9 GB | 8 GB | Latest generation, strong reasoning and coding | Medium |
| **MedGemma** | 5.0 GB | 8 GB | Medical-specific: symptoms, medications, conditions | Medium |
| **DeepSeek-R1 14B** | 9.0 GB | 16 GB | Complex reasoning, detailed analysis, planning | Slower |

> **Recommendation:** Start with **Llama 3.2 3B** (works on any PC). If you have 8+ GB RAM, upgrade to **Mistral 7B** for much better answers. If you have a GPU (NVIDIA/AMD), models run 5-10x faster.

> **Note:** Models are downloaded once and stored permanently. You can have multiple models installed and switch between them in the AI Chat dropdown. Unused models can be deleted from Settings to free disk space.
