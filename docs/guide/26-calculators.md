## 26. Calculators Reference

The Preparedness > Calculators tab contains survival math tools. Here is what each one does:

| Calculator | Inputs | What It Calculates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Water Storage** | People, days, climate | Total gallons needed. Hot climate adds 50%. Includes drinking, cooking, and hygiene water. |
| **Food Storage** | People, days, calories/person/day | Total calories needed. Compares your answer to LDS (2,000 cal) and FEMA (2,400 cal) standards. |
| **Power / Solar Sizing** | Load watts, hours/day, days, sun hours | Total watt-hours, recommended battery capacity (Ah), minimum solar panel wattage. Accounts for 80% depth of discharge and system efficiency. |
| **Watch Schedule** | Team members, shift length, days | Full rotation schedule showing who is on watch when. Ensures equal rest periods. |
| **Solar Position** | Latitude, longitude, date | Sunrise, sunset, solar noon, day length. Useful for planning activities and solar panel orientation. |
| **Moon Phase** | Date | Current moon phase and illumination. A full moon means better visibility at night but also means you are more visible. |
| **Coordinate Converter** | Latitude, longitude | Converts between decimal degrees (38.8977) and degrees-minutes-seconds (38°53\'51.7"N). Useful for radio communication and map reading. |
| **Travel Time** | Distance, mode, terrain | Estimated travel time by foot, bicycle, horse, or vehicle across flat, hilly, mountain, or swamp terrain. Accounts for reduced speed on difficult ground. |
| **Battery Life** | Capacity (mAh), voltage | Watt-hours available and estimated runtime for common devices (flashlight, radio, phone, laptop). |
| **Bleach Calculator** | Bleach %, water clarity, gallons | Exact drops or teaspoons of bleach needed to purify water. Adjusts for cloudy water (double dose). Includes surface disinfection ratios. |
| **Resource Planner** | People, days, activity level | Comprehensive resource needs: water, food (calories), fuel, medical supplies. Cross-references with your actual inventory to show gaps. |

> **Tip:** The Resource Planner is the most powerful calculator — it pulls your real inventory data and shows exactly what you have vs. what you need for a given scenario.
