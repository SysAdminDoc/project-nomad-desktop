## 3. Readiness Score

The Readiness Score is a letter grade (A through F) that measures how prepared you are across 7 categories:

| Category | Max Points | What Improves It |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Water | 20 | Adding water supplies to your inventory |
| Food | 20 | Adding food items, tracking daily usage, removing expired items |
| Medical | 15 | Adding medical supplies, registering patients, recording blood types |
| Security | 10 | Adding cameras, logging access events, resolving incidents |
| Communications | 10 | Adding contacts, logging radio communications |
| Power & Land | 10 | Registering power devices, adding garden plots, saving map waypoints |
| Plans & Knowledge | 15 | Completing checklists, writing notes, uploading documents, running drills |

**Click any category** to jump directly to the relevant Preparedness section where you can improve that score.
