Dose Reference Thresholds
Background: 0.0001-0.001 R/hr (safe indefinitely)
<0.1 R/hr: Acceptable for continuous occupancy
0.1-2 R/hr: Limit exposure. 2 hrs max/day.
2-10 R/hr: Limit to 30 min. Shelter strongly recommended.
10-100 R/hr: Emergency only. Minutes maximum.
>100 R/hr: Lethal zone. Do not enter.
