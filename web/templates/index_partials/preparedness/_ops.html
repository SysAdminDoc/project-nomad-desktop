Surface Disinfection

            

              
General surfaces: 1/3 cup bleach per gallon water. Wipe, let sit 1 min.

              
Norovirus/blood: 1 cup bleach per gallon water. Let sit 10 min.

              
Mold: 1 cup bleach per gallon water. Scrub, let dry completely.

            

            
Use ONLY plain unscented sodium hypochlorite bleach. Never mix bleach with ammonia, vinegar, or other cleaners - toxic gas.
