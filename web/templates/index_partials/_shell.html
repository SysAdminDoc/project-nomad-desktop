Enter Emergency Mode?

Emergency Mode flags this as a live crisis event. It will:

  • Log a critical incident to the timeline
  • Show a persistent banner across every tab
  • Broadcast the state to every open NOMAD window on this machine
  • Record duration on exit for your after-action review

Exit Emergency Mode?

Close the emergency. A closeout entry will be written to the incident timeline with the total duration.
