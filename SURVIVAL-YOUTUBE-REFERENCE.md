| **Canadian Prepper** | Water storage, filtration gear reviews |

| **Primitive Technology** | Primitive clay pot water boiling |
| **Engineer775** | Well systems, water infrastructure |
| **The Provident Prepper** | Long-term water storage |

### Methods to Cover (video topics)
- **Boiling** — 1 min rolling boil (3 min above 5000 ft), hot rock method
- **Filtration** — DIY charcoal/sand/gravel filter, commercial filters (Sawyer, LifeStraw, Berkey)
- **Chemical treatment** — Iodine tablets, chlorine tablets, bleach (8-16 drops/gallon)
- **UV purification** — SteriPEN, solar disinfection (SODIS)
- **Distillation** — Solar still construction, pot distillation
- **Rain collection** — Tarp catchment, barrel systems, gutter collection
- **Natural sources** — Finding water in the wild, dew collection, tree water
